# Privacy and sensitive context

Material sensitive context may be retained with purpose, provenance, epistemic status, confidence, and confidentiality. Internal retention does not authorize external disclosure. Passwords, tokens, one-time codes, reset links, private keys, cookies, and authentication secrets are excluded.

All direct and derived Workspace information is owner-only by default. External disclosure requires fresh, exact, single-use approval for the recipient or destination, channel or action, purpose, information scope, final content or artifact version, attachments, links, visibility, and permissions.
