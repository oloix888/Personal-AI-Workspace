# Community Code of Conduct

We want a useful, candid and respectful community.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas and evidence, not personal worth.
- Be direct without being hostile.
- Respect privacy and do not post other people's sensitive information.
- Disclose limitations and uncertainty.
- Give credit to sources and contributors.
- Help newcomers reproduce and understand problems.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory abuse, doxxing, deliberate deception, sexualized targeting, publication of secrets, or sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock or remove content and restrict participation when necessary to protect the community. Report serious concerns privately to **michal24749@gmail.com**.
