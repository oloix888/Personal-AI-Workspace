## Installation help

Open an issue using the **Installation help** template and include:

- ChatGPT plan or environment
- Available connectors
- Stage where the flow stopped
- Exact error message
- Whether the operation was retried

Do not post passwords, tokens, one-time codes, private mail content or other secrets.
