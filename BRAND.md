# Brand and naming guidance

## Original name

The original project is named **Personal AI Workspace**. Keep this name in attribution and provenance statements.

## Forks and derivatives

You may create forks, adaptations and mutations under the Apache License 2.0. A derivative may have its own distinct name, but should state that it is based on Personal AI Workspace and retain the project `NOTICE`.

## Avoid confusion

Do not describe a modified build as an official Personal AI Workspace release unless it was published from the official repository. Do not imply endorsement by Michał Poliński or Emma ✨.

## Visual direction

Official public materials use burnt orange, amber, warm white and restrained network imagery. Derivative projects may use their own identity while preserving textual attribution.
