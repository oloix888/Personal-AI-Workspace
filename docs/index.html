What can I use it for?

  • Remember important people, relationships, goals, decisions and commitments.
  • Start new conversations with current priorities instead of explaining everything again.
  • Turn conversations into linked tasks, projects, decisions, journal entries and durable knowledge.
  • Keep source-backed personal, business, research, wellbeing and health context in one governed system.
  • Use optional email, calendar, files, GitHub, LinkedIn, analytics and specialist tools when they help.

Why should I use it?

  • Build a growing, structured knowledge base about your whole life: people, goals, work, health, problems, decisions, files and history.
  • During active conversations, ChatGPT proactively evaluates new information and creates or updates the right canonical records when it is materially useful.
  • Turn ideas and obligations into tasks, then track priority, owner, due date, blockers, completion, reviews and follow-ups.
  • With a real scheduled task or automation enabled, receive recurring checks and reminders instead of relying on memory alone.
  • Keep longitudinal wellbeing, mental-health and physical-health context ready for discussion—without pretending to replace qualified professional care.
  • Preserve the full context of recurring problems, attempted solutions, decisions and outcomes so the next conversation starts with evidence.
  • Get better preparation for meetings, messages, business decisions and personal choices while keeping control over data and external actions.

Why it exists

Stop making every AI conversation start from zero.

Important facts, projects, relationships, health context and commitments are usually scattered across conversations and tools. Personal AI Workspace creates a durable, reviewable source of context that can be loaded through approved connectors.

The upcoming Phase 1 release adds Memory Fusion, bounded People in Focus, salient events, a minimum-necessary wellbeing and health snapshot, Autonomous Memory Capture and active workflows for Journal, Observations, Decisions, Projects, Working Notes and Archive.

Architecture

One framework, eight connected layers.

Knowledge

Durable facts, concepts, sources, corrections and typed relationships.

Relations

People, interactions, commitments, context and carefully labelled interpretations.

Decisions

Options, assumptions, outcomes, review dates, patterns and lessons.

Projects & Tasks

A/B/C priorities, project hubs, commitments and verified execution backends.

Journal & Observations

Material events, explicit observations, hypotheses, evidence and counterevidence.

Personal Context

Confidential, dated wellbeing, psychological, health and supplement context with strict epistemic types.

Integrations

Notion at the core; email, calendar, files, GitHub, LinkedIn, analytics and specialist capabilities are optional.

Evolution Lab

Evidence-based operational observations and owner-approved improvements.

Before you install

  1. Have a ChatGPT or Codex account.
  2. Have a Notion account.
  3. Connect the Notion plugin/app to the correct workspace.
  4. Verify that Notion create/edit actions are available.
